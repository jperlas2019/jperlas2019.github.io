Smart Roster

Project information

  • Category: Industry Project
  • Purpose: BCIT Industry Sponsored Student Projects
  • Project date: Sept - Dec 2020
  • Project URL: GitHub Link

Our client approached us with a unique problem which was with their current nurse scheduling system. Every shift, patients require care from nurses at an appropriate skill level. At present, nursing assignments are completed manually which takes up too much time and energy.


Our goal was then to make scheduling more efficient, consistent and reduce the amount of steps within the process as well as removing the need to manually perform this process. The main factor of their problem is time. Making a product that makes their time more efficient will bring great value in the long run.

FEATURES:
  • • Automated Nurse-Patient assignments according to skill, patient needs, and consistency
  • • User friendly interface for a variety of computer literacy levels
  • • Ability to override automated assignments
  • • Automated feedback and alerts when assigning nurses
