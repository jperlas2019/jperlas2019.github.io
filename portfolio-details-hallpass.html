HallPass

Project information

  • Category: UX/UI Design
  • Purpose: BCIT UX/UI Design Course
  • Project date: May 2020
  • Project URL: See the design

A mobile application design concept for a universal school app that links to schools' online services and display information in a familiar, convenient interface.

FEATURES:
  • • An Apple-like interface
  • • Interactive screens and scrolling within prototype
  • • Connects to all post-secondary schools in B.C. (concept)
  • • Able to view deadlines and important dates quickly (concept)
  • • Has basic email functionality, using your school email (concept)
