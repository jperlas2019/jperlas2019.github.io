===================

Music Commands

===================

clear

Clears the queue

loop

Loops the currently playing song

loopoff

Turns off looping

nowplaying

Shows currently playing song

np

Alias for nowplaying

play

Plays music via Youtube, Spotify, Soundcloud links, and direct search. Supports Spotify/Soundcloud tracks, Spotify albums, and Spotify/Youtube public playlists.

Spotify plays music from Youtube. Soundcloud downloads the music locally into PENGU.

p

Alias for play

playnow

Adds song in front of queue

Playlists not supported

pn

Alias for playnow

queue

Shows current music queue.

Attemping to play a song while a song is already playing will automatically put attempted song in queue.

q

Alias for queue

shuffle

Randomizes the entire queue

skip

Skips current song, plays next song in queue.

s

Alias for skip

stop

Stops current song.

Queue is kept the same. Use play or skip to play the next song.

===================

System Commands

===================

connect

PENGU waddles to your voice channel

join

Alias for connect

disconnect

Throw him out.

leave

Alias for disconnect

debug

Tells the secrets of PENGU

help [command]

Displays all commands. If passed with a listed command, shows details.
